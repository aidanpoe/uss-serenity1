1. Introduction

Welcome to USS Serenity, a Star Trek-themed roleplay community. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data in compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR).

Important Distinction: We process two types of data:

  • Real Personal Data: Your Steam account information (including Steam ID64 and username) and system usage data
  • Fictional Roleplay Data: Character profiles and in-game activities (not personal data)

3.2 Fictional Roleplay Data (Not Personal Data)

Character names, ranks, species, departments, medical records, security reports, and all other roleplay content are fictional and not considered personal data under GDPR.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Authentication: Steam ID to verify your identity and manage login sessions
  • Account Management: Username and permissions to control system access
  • Security: Login logs and session management to prevent unauthorized access
  • System Operation: Session data to maintain website functionality
  • Roleplay Activities: Linking your account to fictional character profiles

7. Third-Party Data Sharing

7.1 Steam (Valve Corporation)

We use Steam's authentication service, which involves:

  • Redirecting you to Steam's login page
  • Receiving your Steam ID and profile information
  • Making API calls to Steam servers (located in the United States)

Legal Safeguards: Steam operates under Privacy Shield framework and standard contractual clauses for international data transfers.

7.2 No Other Third Parties

We do not share your data with any other third parties, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media platforms (beyond Steam authentication)
  • No email marketing services

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures:

  • Encryption: HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure Sessions: HTTP-only, secure cookies with CSRF protection
  • Database Security: Prepared statements to prevent SQL injection
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions system
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and vulnerability assessments

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to:

  • Steam Servers (USA): For authentication purposes only
  • Safeguards: Adequate protection mechanisms in place
  • Duration: Only during login process

10. Consent and Legal Basis

Legitimate Interest: We process most data based on legitimate interest in operating a hobby roleplay community.

Consent: By creating an account, you consent to data processing as described in this policy.

Withdrawal: You can withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

11. Age Requirements

You must be at least 13 years old to use this service (Steam's minimum age requirement). If you are under 18, please ensure you have parental consent before creating an account.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that poses high risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

  • Notify relevant authorities within 72 hours
  • Inform affected users without undue delay
  • Provide clear information about the breach and remedial actions

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be:

  • Posted on this page with updated "Last Updated" date
  • Announced to users via system notification
  • Emailed to users for significant changes (if email addresses available)
